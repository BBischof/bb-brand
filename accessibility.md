Generated by `scripts/generate_a11y.py`. Re-run whenever `tokens/tokens.json` changes.

**Thresholds**
- ✅ **AAA** — ≥ 7:1 — enhanced (body text ideal)
- ✅ **AA** — ≥ 4.5:1 — minimum for normal text
- ⚠️ **AA Large** — ≥ 3:1 — minimum for large text (18px+ or 14px bold)
- ❌ **Fail** — below 3:1 — do not use for text

Note: muted and faint text tokens are intentionally lower-contrast and should
only be used for non-essential metadata at 12px+, never for primary content.
